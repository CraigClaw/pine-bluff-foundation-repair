# Pine Bluff Foundation Help Lead Buyer Prospect Plan

## Opportunity

High-ticket structural leads in an older housing market where localized content may compete well against broad regional providers.

## Search Queries Reviewed

- Pine Bluff AR foundation repair contractors
- foundation repair Pine Bluff AR

## Best Buyer Profile

- Local business serving Pine Bluff and Jefferson County.
- Weak website or no city/service page coverage.
- Answers calls quickly.
- Wants foundation repair, crawl space repair, pier installation, concrete leveling leads.
- Will accept tracked calls/forms and give feedback on lead quality.

## Outreach Script

> Hi, is this the right person for new foundation repair jobs? I built a Pine Bluff-focused quote request website for foundation repair, crawl space repair, pier installation, concrete leveling. I am looking for one local company to send the leads to first. Would you be open to taking a few tracked leads as a trial and only paying if the quality makes sense?

## Trial Offer

- 7 days or first 5 qualified leads.
- Qualified lead: call over 60 seconds or form with name, phone, city, project type, and details.
- Start with pay-per-lead by job type, then move to exclusive monthly rental after proof.
