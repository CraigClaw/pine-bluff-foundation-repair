Better quote requests

Details that help a local provider respond faster.

Useful quote details include project type, location, timeline, visible symptoms, approximate size, site access, and whether the request is residential, commercial, emergency, or planning-stage.

This makes each lead more valuable than a bare phone number and helps the buyer decide whether the job fits their crew and service area.

The form is intentionally simple for conversion, but the page copy asks for the details a real business needs before calling back. That balance helps SEO, reduces bad-fit inquiries, and creates a cleaner sales story when pitching the site to a local provider.

After launch, the next improvement should be adding real photos, call notes, and provider feedback so the page can be tuned around the actual jobs that convert.
